The Dispatch

Three guys, a lot of New England, and whatever the weekend looks like.

Out There Co. is Trevor, Tucker, and Jared — three friends from the South Shore who grew up fishing, hiking, and camping all over New England. Fishing is still where most of our time goes, but the channel follows us wherever the weekend lands: a trail, a tent, a chairlift, a tide.

Expect straight talk on gear, Tucker's fly tying, and no filler. No clickbait. No fake reactions. Bad day on the water, quiet morning on the trail, blown-out ski day — that's the video. We're out there either way.
